# Tiny Days Data Deletion

**Effective date:** June 3, 2026

Tiny Days lets users request deletion of their account and related personal data.

## Delete Your Account In The App

If you can access Tiny Days, you can delete your account directly in the app:

1. Open Tiny Days.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Open the **Account** section.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

Deleting your account removes your Tiny Days user profile and removes you from every household where you are a member.

## Request Deletion By Email

If you cannot access the app, email:

[sheepiiHD@gmail.com](mailto:sheepiiHD@gmail.com)

Please send the request from the Google account associated with your Tiny Days account when possible. Include that you are requesting Tiny Days account or data deletion.

## Shared Household Data

Tiny Days is built around shared household records. Deleting your account removes your access and membership from households. Shared household care records may remain available to other authorized household members unless a verified household owner or authorized administrator requests deletion of the shared household data.

## What May Be Deleted

Depending on the request and account verification, deletion may include:

- Your Tiny Days user profile.
- Your household membership.
- Your stored device notification tokens.
- Personal account identifiers associated with your Tiny Days account.

## What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained when necessary for security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, debugging, backup integrity, or when it belongs to shared household records controlled by another authorized household member.
